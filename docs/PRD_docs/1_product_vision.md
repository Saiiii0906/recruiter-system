# SharanAI — Product Vision

## Product Name

**SharanAI**

## Tagline

**Smarter Hiring, Better Decisions**

---

# Vision

SharanAI is an AI-powered recruitment intelligence platform designed to help recruiters, HR teams, and hiring managers evaluate candidates faster, more accurately, and with greater consistency.

Instead of relying solely on manual resume screening, SharanAI combines semantic search, intelligent ranking, candidate analytics, and AI-generated recruiter reports to support better hiring decisions.

The platform aims to reduce recruiter workload while improving candidate quality and hiring efficiency.

---

# Mission

Empower recruitment teams with AI-driven insights that transform hiring from intuition-based decisions into data-driven decisions.

---

# Problem Statement

Recruiters spend significant time reviewing resumes manually.

Current recruitment workflows often involve:

* Manual resume screening
* Keyword-based filtering
* Subjective evaluation
* Lack of centralized candidate analytics
* Difficulty comparing multiple candidates objectively

These limitations increase hiring time and reduce recruitment efficiency.

---

# Solution

SharanAI introduces an AI-powered recruitment workflow that provides:

* Intelligent semantic candidate ranking
* Job Description understanding
* Skill matching
* Candidate profile analytics
* Recruiter reports
* Candidate comparison
* Hiring dashboard

The platform helps recruiters identify the most relevant candidates quickly while maintaining transparency in the evaluation process.

# Core Objectives

The first version of SharanAI focuses on the following capabilities:

* Candidate Ranking
* Candidate Search
* AI Recruiter Reports
* Candidate Analytics
* Job Description Processing
* Dashboard Visualization

Future versions will expand into interview intelligence, AI screening assistants, ATS integrations, and collaborative hiring workflows.

---

# Key Features

## AI Candidate Ranking

Ranks candidates based on semantic similarity and feature engineering.

---

## Candidate Profile

Displays structured candidate information including experience, education, skills, certifications, languages, and profile quality.

---

## AI Recruiter Report

Generates recruiter-friendly summaries that help evaluate candidates efficiently.

---

## Analytics Dashboard

Provides hiring insights such as:

* Candidate volume
* Experience distribution
* Profile quality
* Skills distribution
* Company distribution
* Geographic distribution

---

## Job Description Intelligence

Extracts important hiring information from job descriptions and prepares structured data for candidate ranking.

---

# Product Principles

SharanAI follows these design principles:

* AI should assist recruiters, not replace them.
* Every recommendation should be explainable.
* Interfaces should be clean and distraction-free.
* Performance should remain fast even with large candidate pools.
* Analytics should help decision-making rather than overwhelm users.

---

# Success Metrics

The success of SharanAI will be measured through:

* Reduced candidate screening time
* Improved recruiter productivity
* Higher ranking relevance
* Better hiring decisions
* Positive recruiter experience

---

# Long-Term Vision

SharanAI aims to evolve into a comprehensive AI Recruitment Intelligence Platform capable of supporting end-to-end hiring workflows, advanced candidate evaluation, collaborative recruitment, and enterprise-scale talent intelligence.
